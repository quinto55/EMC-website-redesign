Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Introduction

EMC Tickets ("we," "us") provides ticketing, scanning, marketing, and advertising services for live events. This policy explains what information this website collects and how we use it.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact information you submit through our contact form or by emailing us — typically your name, email address, organization, and message.
  • Usage data — pages visited, approximate location, device and browser characteristics — collected by the analytics tools below only after you accept cookies.
  • Cookies & tracking. With your consent, this site uses PostHog (product analytics and session replay), Microsoft Clarity (usage analytics), the LinkedIn Insight Tag, and the Meta Pixel (advertising measurement). None of these load unless you choose "Accept" on our cookie banner; declining keeps them off entirely.

3. How we use information

To respond to inquiries, operate and improve this website, understand which of our services interest visitors, and measure the effectiveness of our advertising.

4. Sharing & disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the service providers named above (each processing data under their own terms), with professional advisers where required, or where the law requires disclosure.

5. Data retention

Contact inquiries are kept as long as needed to handle your request and our business records. Analytics data is retained per each provider's standard retention settings.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing info@emctickets.com. You can withdraw cookie consent at any time by clearing this site's browsing data, which resets the banner choice.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the date above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy: info@emctickets.com, or EMC Tickets, 8409 Land O Lakes Blvd, Land O Lakes, FL 34638.
